Character Info Photo
Twilight Sparkle

Twilight Sparkle is the central main character in the series. She is a female unicorn, who transforms into an Alicorn later in the series. Twilight represents the element of magic.
Rainbow Dash

Rainbow Dash is a part of the main cast. She is a female unicorn and represents the element of loyalty.
Applejack

She is a female earth pony. Applejack is one of the main characters and represents the element of honesty.

Rarity

Rarity is a female unicorn, a part of the Mane 6, who represents the element of generosity.
Pinkie Pie

Pinkie pie is a female earth pony, who represents the element of laughter. She is one of the main characters
Fluttershy

She is a pegasus, representing element of kindness. Fluttershy is a member of the Mane 6.
